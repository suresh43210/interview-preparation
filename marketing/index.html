💡 Bank Marketing Tips — AI ले दिने सुझावहरू

Siddhartha Bank को लागि Effective Marketing Strategies

🌙

Festival-Based Campaigns

Dashain, Tihar, New Year मा special loan offers र FD bonuses announce गर्नुस्। Nepali customers को festival spending pattern अनुसार campaigns बनाउनुस्।

📱

Digital-First Approach

Facebook र TikTok मा short video content — "30 seconds मा Home Loan apply गर्ने तरिका।" Young professionals लाई target गर्नुस् YouTube ads बाट।

🤝

Referral Marketing

Existing customers लाई नयाँ customer refer गर्दा gift, extra FD rate, वा cashback दिनुस्। Word-of-mouth Nepal मा सबैभन्दा powerful marketing हो।

📊

Educational Content

"कसरी home loan लिने?", "FD vs Savings — कुन राम्रो?" — यस्ता educational posts ले trust build गर्छ र organic reach बढाउँछ।

✈️

NRN Community Target

Remittance सेवाको promotion Qatar, UAE, Korea, Malaysia मा रहेका Nepalis लाई। Siddhartha Bank मार्फत पैसा पठाउँदा कम charge को campaign।

🌾

Agriculture Loan Drive

Farming season (Asar, Kartik) मा agriculture loan को awareness। Rural branches बाट targeted SMSes र local radio मा advertisements।
